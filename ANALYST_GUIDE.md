# GeoTrace Forensics X — Analyst Guide

1. Start in **Analyst** mode.
2. Import images or a folder.
3. Review the Evidence Queue and Decision Rail.
4. For maps/screenshots, open **Map Workspace** and confirm whether the anchor is native GPS, derived coordinate, OCR clue, or weak place label.
5. Use Manual Crop OCR on map search bars, coordinates, bottom labels, route cards, and visible chat/location text.
6. Treat filename timestamps and screenshot-derived coordinates as leads until corroborated.
7. Open Reports and click **Preview Export**.
8. Generate the Shareable Redacted package unless the audience is internal.
9. Verify the package and keep the manifest/signature/verification files with the handoff.
