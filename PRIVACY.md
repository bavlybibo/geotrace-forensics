# Privacy Policy

GeoTrace Forensics X is designed as an offline-first desktop forensic triage tool.

## Local processing

By default, evidence files, OCR text, extracted metadata, generated reports, hashes, and case snapshots are processed locally on the analyst machine. The application does not intentionally upload evidence, images, OCR text, locations, or report contents to any remote service.

## Optional components

Some optional third-party tools may be installed by the user, such as Tesseract OCR or optional AI/ML packages. Users are responsible for reviewing the privacy behavior and licensing of any external component they install. Remote AI integrations are disabled by default and must not be enabled for sensitive evidence unless the user has explicit authorization and a lawful basis.

## Export modes

GeoTrace includes multiple export modes. Shareable and courtroom-redacted exports are intended to reduce exposure of raw paths, previews, URLs, OCR text, usernames, emails, and location details. Analysts must still review exported packages before external sharing.

## Evidence responsibility

Users are responsible for handling evidence according to their organization, legal requirements, chain-of-custody rules, and applicable privacy laws.
